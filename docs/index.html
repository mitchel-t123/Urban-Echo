Symptom Check

Sound familiar?

If you ticked yes to more than one of these, you're not alone — it's the most common diagnosis we see across Perth schools.

01

Endless school events

School events, sports carnivals, presentation nights, graduations — the calendar never stops, and someone has to run sound for all of it.

02

No in-house sound guy

You're relying on whoever's free, not whoever's trained. That's a lot of pressure to put on a teacher with a spare period.

03

Too many school events

Weekly, fortnightly, whole-school, year-group — they add up fast, and each one needs someone behind the desk.

04

No time

You've got a school to run. Cables, mic checks and EQ settings shouldn't be eating into your actual job.

05

Tired of feedback

Literally and figuratively. Feedback belongs in staff meetings — not screeching through the PA mid-speech.

06

Always multi-tasking

Running the projector, the mic, the slides and the program at once isn't sustainable — and it shows.
